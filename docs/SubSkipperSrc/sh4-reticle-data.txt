The meaning of the reticle marks is outlined in the table below:

Optic Used-----------(Zoom Level)----------------Small Marks-------------Large Marks
Periscope------------(Low Magnification)-----------1°-------------------------4°
Periscope------------(High Magnification)----------0.25°(0°15’)---------------1°
TBT-----------------------(7x)---------------------0.2° (0°12’)---------------1°
